Applications of AI

There are many branches of AI, and for each type of AI there are many different uses for them.

NLP

  • Language translate applications, like Google Translate.
  • Word processors like Microsoft Word, and Grammarly that employ NLP, to check grammatical errors.
  • Interactive Voice Response, or (IVR) applications used in call centers to respond to some users' requests
  • Personal assistants application like Ok Google, Cortona, Alexa, and Siri.

Computer Vision

  • Autonomous Vehicles, such as Tesla cars.
  • Facial Recognition, used in modern-day devices, to help log into your device.
  • Gaming and Controls
  • Surveilence, AI can also be used to capture criminals through surveilence.
